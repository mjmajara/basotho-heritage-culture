TRADITONAL CLOTHING OF BASOTHO

Basotho traditional dress in a very special way that characterized by colorful Seshoeshoe dresses, vibrant blankets, and the distinctive conical straw hat, the Mokorotlo. Women wear seshoeshoe fabric in various designs and colors, often with blankets around their waists. Men wear kobo (blankets) and the conical hat as national attire. Older garments include sefaha sa letsopa (clay bead necklaces) for young girls and tseha (sheepskin undergarments) for boys
